How We Work – A Step-by-Step Guide to Our Service

At LS BodyShop, we believe in a clear and transparent process from start to finish. Here’s how we handle each project to ensure the best possible service for you:

1

Contact Us

Reach out to us via phone, email, or our online contact form. We’ll discuss your needs and provide initial advice on the best way forward for your vehicle.

Schedule an Appointment

Once we’ve talked, we’ll schedule a convenient time for you to visit our workshop. Our team is flexible and works around your schedule to find the best time for the inspection.

2
3

Visit the Workshop & Damage Inspection

Bring your car to our workshop, where our expert technicians will carry out a thorough inspection of the damage or issues with your vehicle. This allows us to assess the situation and determine the best course of action for repairs.

Estimate, Approval & Start of Work

After the inspection, we’ll provide a detailed estimate for the work required. Once you approve the quote, we’ll get started on the repairs right away, ensuring your vehicle is in the best hands.
